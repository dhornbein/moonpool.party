How it Works

Moon Pools take place on weekdays 3 Hours (tradionally 8 am - 11 am, but this might change). Each daily session consists of three sprints.

Daily Flow

Each day will consist of a short opening ritual where you will share your intentions for the day and request and coordinate any help you might need.

Each sprint will be about 40-50 minutes, this is your time to focus on what matters. Breakout rooms are available for any small groups that want to form or session offerings.

Between sprints will be breaks (~10 min) where you are invited to share a movement exercise with the group.

After the 3rd sprint will be a closing ceremony.

Cost

The Moon Pool is offered as a certain kind of gift. This is not like fruit from the trees, it is not given with no expectation. This is the kind of gift that patiently waits for a gift in return that's worth just a bit more. The Moon Pool expects to be indebted to you upon receiving your gift.

This is what keeps the pool filled.
